How do I manage what other people can see?

By default, Subproject sets all of your information to private.

You can change the privacy settings of your worklogs in the Worklog Settings panel. By making your worklogs public, they can be seen by anyone who visits your work page. If a project is managed by someone else, they may be able to see work you have logged on that project even if your worklogs are private.

The privacy of your tasks and projects can be controlled individually, by going to the edit page of the project or task you would like to change the privacy of. By default (unless you have chosen otherwise), all tasks and projects you create are private. Projects and tasks are always visible to the people you have added as members or assignees.

Tags you create are private and are not shown on any projects you add them to. You can make a tag public by going to its edit page and checking the "public" checkbox. If you would like to limit who can see your tags, you can add other users as viewers (that is, they are able to see your tag and anything tagged with it that you have also allowed them to see) and set the tag to private. You can also allow other people to use your tags (that is, they are able to see them and add/remove them to more things) by adding them as tag users.
